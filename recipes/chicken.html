Chicken Recipe

Description

This is a simple roast chicken that is done by marinating beforehand and cooked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 sprig of rosemary
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Wash the chicken and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Stuff the cavity with the lemon, rosemary, and garlic.
  5. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and rub the butter all over the skin.
  6. Roast the chicken in the oven for 1 hour and 30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F.
  7. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes
