Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a classic pasta carbonara that is made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 ounces of pancetta
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, whisk together the eggs, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Cook the pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy.
  5. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook until fragrant.
  6. Drain the pasta, reserving 1 cup of the cooking water.
  7. Add the pasta to the skillet and toss to combine.
  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the egg mixture, tossing to coat the pasta.
  9. Add the reserved cooking water as needed to loosen the sauce.
