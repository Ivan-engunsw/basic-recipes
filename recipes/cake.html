Cake Recipe

Description

This is a classic yellow cake that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.
  7. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients alternately with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
